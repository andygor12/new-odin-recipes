Instant Noodles

Home

Description

Everybody love instant noodles. They are fast and convenient. Anybody can make a bowl of good tasty instant noodles.

In this tutorial, I will show you how to add an extra layer of pizzazz to your instant noodles!

Ingredients

  • Instant noodles of your choosing
  • 1 Pasteurized egg
  • Green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the instant noodle into a cooking pot.
  2. Add 500ml of water.
  3. Put the lid on and cook until boil.
  4. Add seasonings + egg into the pot.
  5. Stir until the egg is mixed evenly.
  6. Pour noodles out in a bowl and add green onions to finish the dish.
