Boiled Eggs

Home

Description

Boiled eggs are extrememly healthy. They are rich in protein and easy to make.

In this tutorial, I will show you how to boil the perfect boiled eggs with your rice cooker!

Ingredients

  • 2 Pasteurized eggs.
  • Kitchen grade paper towels.

Steps

  1. Grab a piece of kitchen paper towel and damp it.
  2. Place the paper towel in the rice cooker.
  3. Place the 2 pasteurized eggs on top of the wet paper towel.
  4. Start the rice cooker and set a timer for 12 minutes.
  5. After 12 minutes, remove the eggs from the rice cooker and place them in a bowl of cool water.
  6. There you have it, 2 perfectly cooked eggs. Runny and yummy egg yolks awaits you!
