Fried Eggs

Home

Description

Fried eggs are extremely delicious. They are crunchy and compliments very well with a lot of dishes.

In this tutorial, I will show you have to make the perfect fried egg!

Ingredients

  • 2 Pasteurized eggs.
  • Olive oil. Healthy.

Steps

  1. Heat the frying pan to the point where splashing a drop of water will make them instantly evaporate.
  2. Pour the olive oil onto the pan enough to cover the surface of the frying pan.
  3. Crack the eggs onto the frying pan.
  4. Cook each side for 30 seconds and the flip them evenly.
  5. Cook the other side for 30 seconds.
  6. Turn off the stove and there you have it. Unseasoned fried eggs...
